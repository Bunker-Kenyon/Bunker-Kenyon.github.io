Geek Squad

Geek Squad was a trial by fire. It was fun but high stress. I had to be both a PC repair technician and salesperson. Here I honed my customer service skills and PC/Mac repair skills. To keep up with the ever-changing consumer electronics world, it was a face paced learning environment. I was constantly in training of some kind. Here I also had my first taste of leadership. I was the lead tech. I had to train new techs, coach, and go over metrics with employees. I also participated in many pilot or test programs to see if a particular new strategy would work. Of the 12 techs we had I consistently brought in 50% or more of the revenue for the tech bench. My Geek Squad was #2 out of 150+ locations in the nation. Although I find some aspect of sales and customer service stressful, I am good at it. My secret is honesty and kindness.
